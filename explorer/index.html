The operating route

A deliberately unsafe request—enable remote writes by default—shows the complete decision path rather than hiding behind a safe happy-path task.

  1. 01 · PrimaryClassify and bound

    One independent lane; parent keeps write and claim ownership.

  2. 02 · ScoutRead-only evidence

    Two files, one lane, no write, no recursion.

  3. 03 · ReviewerChallenge the proposal

    Separate pass identifies the broken fixture assertion.

  4. 04 · ToolVerify 13 fixture assertions

    Local, deterministic, network disabled.

  5. 05 · PrimarySynthesize

    Keep remote write off; test read-only first.

  6. 06 · HumanReject / defer / approve

    Records intent only; executes nothing external.
